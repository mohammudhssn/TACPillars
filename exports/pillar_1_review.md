# SUB-PILLAR 1.1: Foundational Sciences for Anesthesia Practice

**Definition:** The scientific foundation underpinning all clinical anesthesia practice, including human physiology and pathophysiology, pharmacology and pharmacokinetics, applied anatomy, and the chemistry and physics relevant to anesthesia delivery. This sub-pillar encompasses the basic science knowledge required for understanding drug behavior, physiologic responses, and disease processes that inform clinical decision-making.
