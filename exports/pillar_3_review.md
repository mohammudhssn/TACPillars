## Pillar Definition

The non-human systems that make anesthesia possible: facilities, OR operations, workflows, IT, cybersecurity, anesthesia machines, monitors, supply chain, turnover processes, and documentation systems. This pillar maps both the physical and digital infrastructure that enables safe anesthesia delivery and the operational systems that drive efficiency and quality.

# SUB-PILLAR 3.1: Physical Infrastructure and Facility Systems
