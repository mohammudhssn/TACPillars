## Pillar Definition

All aspects of career lifecycle, job transitions, workforce trends, leadership development, organizational behavior, conflict, recruitment, retention, locums, W2 vs 1099 pathways, team dynamics, and professional advancement. This pillar maps both the macro forces that shape CRNA work and the micro realities of daily professional life.
